Key Features

Obstacle Detection

Equipped with sensors that detect nearby objects and guide the robot to avoid collisions.

AI-Driven Camera

Camera with AI programming allows the robot to recognize and respond to its environment for smoother navigation.

Wireless Tracking

The robot follows a WiFi beacon, enabling remote destination control without the need for manual operation.
