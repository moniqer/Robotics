Specifications

Materials

  • 1 Breadboard
  • 1 VEX Brain
  • 4 VEX Motors
  • 1 ESP32 Camera
  • 1 V5 Robot Battery Li-ion 1100 mAh
  • 4 VEX Ultrasonic Range Sensors
  • 1 V5 Robot Radio
  • Various steel plates, chassis rails, screws, and wheels

Operating Instructions

  1. Turn on the power button of the brain (ensure battery is charged).
  2. Open the project file on the robot's interface.
  3. Click "Run" and place the robot on the ground.
  4. Once at the target location, click "Stop." Ensure the WiFi connection is stable.
